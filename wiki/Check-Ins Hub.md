## Check-In Format & Philosophy

### Call 02 (Klaus Hosting)
**Format**: Open check-in, no specific prompt
**Philosophy**: Everyone sharing updates freely

### Call 06 (Jerry's Structured Format)
**Rules established**:
1. No traffic cop - volunteers step forward
2. Check in only once
3. No comments back and forth during check-ins
4. Moratorium on chat during check-ins (can save but don't send)
5. Pauses are appreciated - like Quaker Meeting
6. Be moderately brief - check in on something OGM-y
7. Don't empty entire brain of everything banked for the week

**Philosophy**: "Quiet moments are not awkward, they're appreciative"

**Influence**: Quaker Meeting silence and reflection

### Call 14 (Evolved Format)
**Format**: Check-in round, then open conversation
**Flow**: Structured check-ins ‚Üí Emergent conversation (AI bubble dominated)
