- Them: "No. Did you vote? No, we just don't care. We're just not interested."
- Alex reaction: **"Profoundly shocked me that there are people in this world that are like that. So incurious."**

**AI Learning Breakthrough**:
- Problem: "Could never understand when teacher told me something - comprehension not quite right"
