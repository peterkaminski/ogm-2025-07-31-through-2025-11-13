### Phase 3: THE REVELATION (Call 14 - November 6)

**MOST SIGNIFICANT MOMENT**: OGM as Gravitational Center

**Tab Groups Life-Changing Discovery**:
- Problem: 250 tabs open across multiple browser windows, laptop overwhelmed
- "What do I do now" feeling
- Discovery: Browser tab groups feature - never knew what it was called or where to find it
- Transformation: Able to sort and bucket tabs into groups, close groups, close browser windows
- Freed up machine resources: "Poor little laptop could function again"
- Things started to flow, not be in overwhelm
- **"This is life-changing for someone like me"**

**Core Beliefs Revealed**:
- Every human being has value to add and contribute
- What do each one of those individuals need in order to be enabled to contribute?
- How do we meet everyone's needs?
- Everything else substantially irrelevant if not germane to those inquiries

**Capacity Unleashed**:
- Tab groups unleashed "flood tide of capacity"
- Organizing, moving into action, unleashing the hounds

**THE OGM REVELATION**:
- "Touching bunch of projects - OGM people or people that have dipped in and out"
- Starting to coalesce - two completely independent projects connect
- Doug aware of both, aware of how they complement and fit larger context
- **"List of about 12 projects, 60% have OGM in common"**
- **"Hat tip to Jerry: Being the energetic center of gravity out of which a lot of these things connected with, met, and now coalesceable"**

**Impact on Jerry**:
This arrived at Call 14, the same call as Jerry's first psilocybin journey. External validation (Doug's revelation) converged with internal transformation (Jerry's journey). Jerry had been frustrated in Call 09 that OGM was "lovely salons" without impact. Doug showed the impact WAS happening - as hidden networked connections, not visible bodies of work.

**Data Center Apocalypse**:
- Rate at which energy technology, battery technology, quantum computing progressing
- Massive billion-dollar data center investments obsolete in 18 months
- "On intuitive logical level, it doesn't make sense. It's gotta blow up. When it's too good to be true, it defies reality."
- Biggest players canning 30, 40, 50K people to divert resource to constructing data centers
- **"That's the end. Not the beginning of the end. Not a bubble. That's the end of whatever this has been."**
- "Reached its insane logical conclusion as a capitalist train run amok"
- "Don't know what's after that, but I do know what's ending. Thank God. It needed to go away. We need to do us differently and better if we're not gonna go extinct."

**Significance**:
- Doug's "gravitational center" insight validated Jerry's entire OGM project
- Showed impact was networked/mycelial, not centralized/visible
- Perfect convergence with Jerry's psilocybin "mycelial networks" experience
- Data center observation showed systems-level apocalyptic thinking
