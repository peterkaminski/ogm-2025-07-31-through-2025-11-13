### Act 3: Finding Purpose Without Sadness (Call 06 - September 4)

**Key Realization**:
> "Can work with people on problems **without being down in sadness**"

> "River Arts District artists determined to work in live-work lofts, **not sad**. Some people can be around sad, Kevin couldn't."

**New approach**: Working without federal government, looking at community investment funds

**Evolution**: Progressing from pure trauma (Calls 01-02) to finding constructive role while avoiding direct exposure to sadness
