### Phase 4: Development and Refinement (Calls 08-16)

**Call 14** (Nov 6): Progress Report on "Food with Thought"

**Environmental Benefits as Second Crop**:
- Farmer has food crop + environmental benefit crop linked together
- Example: Mississippi Delta - reduce nitrogen runoff (easy to measure)
- Incentives sufficient for conservative farmers to adopt
- **Leverage point**: By building secondary revenue stream, all other things fall into place
  - Soil health
  - Cover crops
  - No-till practices
  - Crop rotation

**Market Reality**:
- "How easy it would be to make an impact, and how stubbornly resistant the market is to engage in change"
- CSAs + farmers markets = only 3% of total sales
- Need to get these farmers into wholesale market
- Bundling and aggregating required

**The Trigger** (Union of Concerned Scientists):
- "Yet another last urgent warning" - passing climate tipping points
- Will be ignored (as always)
- Missing piece: Transition to "what can you actually do about this?"

**Agriculture of the Middle**:
- Small to medium-sized farmers can't get into market
- Food probably most damaging part of our interaction with natural world
- Destroying watersheds, soils, biodiversity
- But: Has capacity to recover, heal, regenerate

**Large Company Threat**:
- Monsanto, Nestle automating, "climate smart technology"
- Hugely capital intensive
- Will exclude more farmers
