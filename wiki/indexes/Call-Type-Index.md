## Patterns by Call Type

### Hybrid Calls Tend To...
- Introduce major frameworks (Three Horizons, Intimacy Gradients, Speed Round)
- Explore big questions ("What is X?")
- Balance intellectual exploration with personal experience
- Generate ongoing themes that recur across calls

**Best For**:
- Understanding recurring themes
- Seeing framework introductions
- Following definitions obsession pattern
- Tracking thematic evolution

---

### Check-In Calls Tend To...
- Produce breakthrough moments (Kevin's miracle, Jerry's validation, Doug's realization)
- Reveal hidden patterns (OGM as gravitational center)
- Show personal transformations (Jerry's psilocybin, Klaus's transition)
- Demonstrate community support in action

**Best For**:
- Understanding participant story arcs
- Witnessing major revelations
- Seeing community bonds
- Following personal journeys

**Pattern**: **Major insights emerge in check-in calls** (Calls 06, 11, 14)

---

### Topical Calls Tend To...
- Address specific crises or urgent topics
- Focus group energy on single issue
- Generate action (Hurricane response)
- Set up later breakthroughs (Call 15 → Call 16)

**Best For**:
- Understanding specific issues deeply
- Seeing OGM crisis response
- Finding focused discussions
- Conceptual frameworks and theory
